Care Continuity Plan

Pet insurance often ends when the owner passes away. PawLegacy ensures continuous care funding beyond insurance limitations.

Pet insurance does not transfer automatically after the owner's death. Coverage may be lost when it is needed most.

2️⃣ Trust Coverage

Your pet trust fund ensures medical, food, and long-term care costs are always covered — even without insurance.

3️⃣ Emergency Buffer

Built-in contingency funds handle unexpected surgeries, chronic conditions, and aging-related care.
